Bard

Your personal magical conductor

Bard is an interactive audioscape tool. Bring your own sounds and let Bard paint an auditory landscape, adapting smoothly to your commands. Perfect for table-top RPGs.

Here be dragons

Bard is currently alpha software, which means there are bugs, and the user interface needs a lot of improvement. Despite this, it is possible to create epic audioscapes. I encourage you to try it out and see what you think.

I'm very keen to hear your thoughts, ideas, and questions. You can join the Discord community or drop a line to cheery.idea2972@fastmail.com (the email looks weird because it's an alias that forwards to my personal address).

Note that Bard is entirely free to use, and will likely remain so for the foreseeable future. In any case you'll never have to pay to use any of the versions currently available for download.
